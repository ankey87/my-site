Meet Amanda

Amanda recently left the teaching profession to pursue a career in tech.
Amanda began attending Kenzie Academy in July 2019 to study UX/UI
Engineering. She completed her first six months in January and has
received her UX Design Certificate. She began her UI studies in January
and is excited to receive her UX/UI Engineering Certificate in July.

Amanda is 32 years old and has two beautiful children. Aria is 8 and
Ezra is 22 months. When she is not studying or working at her job with
Southern Legacy Marketing & Design, she loves to spend time with her family.
Other hobbies include reading, video games, and photography.
